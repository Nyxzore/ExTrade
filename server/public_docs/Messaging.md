# Private & Secure Messaging

Your privacy is our priority. ExoTrade features a robust messaging system built for security.

## Features
- **End-to-End Encryption**: Your conversations remain between you and the other party.
- **Listing Integration**: Easily share and discuss specific listings within the chat.
- **Media Support**: Send photos and attachments securely.
- **Read Receipts**: Know when your messages have been delivered and read.

Communication is key to successful [[Marketplace]] trades.
